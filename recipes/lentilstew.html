Lentil Stew

Description

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Onions, carrots, red pepper, spinach
  • Seasoning
  • Vegetable Broth

Steps to Make Lentil Stew

  1. Cut onions, carrots, red peppers, and spinach into small cubes.
  2. Saute vegetables on medium heat for five minutes
  3. Soak your lentils!
  4. Put the lentils in the dish, immediately pour in the vegetable broth.
  5. Let simmer until lentils are tender. Season accordingly.
